Website Launch

March 13, 2025

Welcome to my new website! After months of planning and design, I'm excited to finally share my photography and creative work with you.

This platform will serve as a hub for my latest projects, photography collections, and creative explorations. I'll be regularly updating the content with new photos, stories, and insights into my creative process.

The design of this website reflects my minimalist aesthetic, focusing on clean lines, thoughtful typography, and a user experience that puts the content first.

New York Photography Series

March 10, 2025

I'm thrilled to announce my latest photography series capturing the essence of New York City. This collection explores the contrast between the city's architectural marvels and its quiet, contemplative spaces.

The series features images from Central Park, the High Line, and various neighborhoods that showcase the unique character of the city. Each photograph aims to present a fresh perspective on familiar landmarks and discover beauty in unexpected places.

Stay tuned for the full gallery, which will be available in the Photography section soon.

Upcoming Exhibition

March 5, 2025

I'm pleased to share that a selection of my work will be featured in an upcoming exhibition titled "Urban Minimalism" at the Contemporary Art Gallery in Taipei.

The exhibition will run from April 15 to May 30, 2025, and will showcase photographs that explore the intersection of urban landscapes and minimalist aesthetics.

If you're in Taipei during this period, I'd love for you to visit the exhibition. More details about the opening reception and special events will be posted here soon.
